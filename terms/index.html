2) How the Service works (summary)

Event organizers upload event photos to our backend. Guests scan a QR code at the venue, open our landing page, verify their WhatsApp number via OTP, and then proceed to the client page to capture a live selfie. Our system computes embeddings and performs a face-recognition match to surface photos that likely include the guest, which the guest can view and download.
